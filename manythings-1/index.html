Winged Victory

Directed by Giacomo Jay Ganduglia

Based on a series of concept art created by the director in 2015, Winged Victory is a short spec commercial, and it explores the image of the woman as symbol of victory . Following a short conceptual and stylized vision of the topic, celebrating what . H. W. Janson described it as "the greatest masterpiece of Hellenistic sculpture".
