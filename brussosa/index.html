Brussosa SS21 campaign - Raíces

Directed by Somayeh Jafari

Brussosa SS21 campaign infuses elements of city life with nature, bringing the ethos of this Spanish brand alive. Raíces (Roots) is a film focusing on natural materials used in the designing of Brussosa bags whilst maintaining the city heritage of the brand based in Barcelona.
