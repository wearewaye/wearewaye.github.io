01.WHO’S WAYE?

WAYE (We Are Your Enemy) is a Film Productions and Creative Collective established in 2018.

Founded by award winning filmmaker Federica Schiavello and visual artist Giacomo Jay Ganduglia, WAYE has been consistently working with independent artists and projects over the years, spanning from short and feature films,, documentaries, music videos, live art, advertisement and more.

Our films have been screened and shot around the world’s stages and festivals, diving into the heart and soul of filmmaking and art-making.

WAYE has many collaborators around the world, thriving through exchange of ideas, skills and ultimately, a love for storytelling.
