01.WHO’S WAYE?

Waye is a creative duo based in North London. It is founded by Jay & Fed but embraces many talented beings across our creative projects.

Waye focuses on narrative factual or fictional content, generally short, but we’d love also to produce long-form.

Waye is a baby between a Production Company and an Artistic Collective thanks to the personality and skills of its founders. Waye starts from the idea, the heart by then developing in bureaucracy, budgets, tasks and pre-production, arriving then to filming and ending through the post-production process.

We are a comprehensive institution from the begging to the end, and what we don’t have in our arsenal, we can outsource with our Waye talented contributors.

We create short films, music videos, documentaries, live-art videos, advertisement for small businesses and more! The most important thing is to give justice to that message and make something meaningful.

So, if you are looking for a more open and creative approach and you need someone to help you create cool content, your next music video or the whole identity of your brand, drop us a line, and get in touch.
