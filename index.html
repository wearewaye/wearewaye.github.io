01.WHO’S WAYE?

WAYE (also know as "Wayerism" or "those weird italians drinking coffee all the time") is a creative collective focusing on filmmaking, creative development, illustration and more.

We are proud of our Working Class background and striving to
become the first working class production company without posh/hip faff.

It is founded by the creatives Fed (Federica Schiavello) & Jay (Giacomo J Ganduglia). Waye starts from the idea, the heart, then organising the paperwork, budgets, tasks in production. We then take it to set and finally to our comprehensive post-production pipeline process.

We create short films, music videos, documentaries, live-art videos, advertisement for businesses and more!
Ultimately, the most important goal is to give justice to that message you carry within your project and make something meaningful.

If you are looking for an open and creative approach, or you need someone to help you create cool content,
your next music video or the whole identity of your brand, drop us a line, and get in touch, yo!
