01.WHO’S WAYE?

Waye is a baby between a Production Company and an Artistic Collective based in North London.

It is founded by the creatives Fed (Federica Schiavello) & Jay (Giacomo Ganduglia). Waye starts from the idea, the heart, then developing in bureaucracy, budgets, tasks and pre-production, arriving ultimately filming and a comprehensive post-production process.  We are a comprehensive institution from the begging to the end, and what we don’t have in our arsenal, we can outsource with our Waye talented contributors.

We create short films, music videos, documentaries, live-art videos, advertisement for small businesses and more! Especially, we’d love to transition to long-form content too. Finally, the most important thing is to give justice to that message and make something meaningful.

So, if you are looking for an opened and creative approach either you need someone to help you create cool content, your next music video or the whole identity of your brand, drop us a line, and get in touch, yo!
